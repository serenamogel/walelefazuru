    AEW Collision Results (12/23/23):Who Advances In The Blue League Of The Continental Classic?

    This weeks episode of AEW Collision skips the promos and goes right into in-ring action in the Blue League of the Continental Classic. A video package plays recapping the Gold League of the AEW Continental Classic.

    06 Jul 2024, 00:00

    This week’s episode of AEW Collision skips the promos and goes right into in-ring action in the Blue League of the Continental Classic.

    • Bryan Danielson and Claudio Castagnoli are the first two men to get a draw in this tournament.

    A video package plays recapping the Gold League of the AEW Continental Classic.

    • Max Caster gets a rollup with a handful of tights on Action Andretti after a missed standing Shooting Star Press to retain the titles for his team.
    • Keith Lee hits Brian Cage with the Big Bang Catastrophe to score the pinfall victory.

    Lee gets on a microphone and says almost a year ago; someone took him out for two months with a cinderblock. Keith Lee says the person isn’t getting the hint so he’s coming to Dynamite to make his intentions clear and intends to tear down his house brick by brick.

    Toni Storm and Mariah May are backstage. Storm doesn’t know who May is despite them being together for the past month and helping her out Wednesday on Dynamite. May says her American wrestling license has been approved, and she will be competing soon. May asks Storm for advice, and Storm says she isn’t paying attention and is only focused on the camera. Storm runs down Riho for their upcoming match at AEW World’s End.

    Christian Cage speaks on AEW Collision

    The AEW TNT Champion Christian Cage and Nick Wayne come out. Christian Cage says he’s not going to explain Shayna Wayne’s actions when she can do it herself and brings her to the ring. Shayna says she did what she did because of what Adam Copeland did to her son. Shayna says she did what any loving mother would do and protected her son. The crowd boos. Shayna says the one person who loves and cares for her son as much as she does is Christian Cage.

    Christian Cage says Shayna realizes what he’s always known and that’s Adam Copeland is a piece of crap. Cage says if anyone should have empathy for Shayna, it should be Copeland because he only grew up with a single mother. Cage says Montreal wasn’t a great night for Copeland, but it was a great night for him. Christian implies he had sex with Shayna Wayne after he won in Montreal.

    Cage accepts Copeland’s match for AEW World’s End, but doesn’t accept it as a challenge because Copeland isn’t a challenge for him. Cage says he will put Copeland down at the pay-per-view for the last time.

    Ricky Starks and Big Bill are backstage. Bill says he thinks Kenny Omega’s injury sounds made up, and he’s just afraid to face them for the AEW Tag Team Titles at World’s End. Jericho arrives and says Omega is out, but he’s not forfeiting anything. Jericho says he’ll find a partner and take those titles from Starks and Big Bill.

    A huge upset in the AEW Continental Classic

    • Daniel Garcia gets a flash pin on Brody King to score the pinfall victory.

    The lights go out after the match, and when they come back on, all three members of House of Black are in the ring. Daddy Magic gets in the ring, but Buddy Matthews takes him out. FTR comes out to make the save. FTR challenges House of Black to a match. It’s not specified when it will take place.

    • Thunder Rosa hits the Tijuana Bomb on Skye Blue to score the pinfall victory for her team.
    • Eddie Kingston hit Andrade El Idolo with two spinning back fists and a swinging suplex to score the pinfall victory.

    Bryan Danielson comes out and stares down Eddie Kingston in the ring as AEW Collision goes off the air.
