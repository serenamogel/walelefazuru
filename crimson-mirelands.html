    From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

    The Crimson Mirelands (Japanese: 紅蓮の湿地 Crimson Wetlands) are a large area in Hisui. It is home to a Noble Hisuian Lilligant and Ursaluna. All Burmy that battle here will turn into their Sandy Cloak form.

    In the future, the Crimson Mirelands become parts of eastern Sinnoh, including the Great Marsh and Lake Valor.

    Trivia

    • The Crimson Mirelands' Hisui Pokédex sublisting is the only one without Stantler, Machop, or their evolutionary relatives.
    • The music that plays in the Crimson Mirelands features motifs from various themes from Pokémon Diamond and Pearl and their remakes. This was confirmed in the Pokémon LEGENDS Arceus Super Music Collection.
      • "Crimson Mirelands 1-1", which plays during midday while in most locations of the Crimson Mirelands, does not feature any motifs. However, upon defeating the Noble Lilligant, "Crimson Mirelands 1-2" instead plays, which is essentially the same as the former theme, but with features of Routes 205 and 206's respective themes added in.
      • The second theme that could play during midday, "Crimson Mirelands 2-1", is a remix of Eterna City and Solaceon Town's respective themes. Upon the defeat of Noble Lilligant, "Crimson Mirelands 2-2" instead plays, which is largely the same as the former remix, and does not add in any additional motifs.
      • The music that plays in Lake Valor, is a remix of the same theme that plays in Sinnoh's Lakes in Pokémon Diamond and Pearl, and it's remakes.
      • The music that plays in the Diamond Clan Settlement, is a remix of Oreburgh City's theme.
