    Details of Jake Wallin’s Obituary Are Examined

    Jake Wallin Funeral To Be Held

    Jake Wallin will be laid to rest on Saturday, July 22 at 10:30 a.m. at Pequot Lakes High School, 30805 Olson Street, Pequot Lakes, Minnesota 56472.

    Jake’s colleagues, family, fellow officers, and community members will be able to pay their respects at his funeral.

    Officer Jake Wallin’s body will be escorted by the Fargo Police Department along a two-and-a-half-hour route to Pequot Lakes in a solemn and poignant funeral procession.
