    Lifestyle and Vacations

    If you have noticed the sizzling physique of Kawhi Leonard, you must also know that the man works really hard to achieve it. Kawhi is an avid fitness enthusiast, and incorporating a balanced diet, and a proper exercise routine has really paid him well.

    To details, Leonard especially focuses on his core and eventually includes every other part of his body. He regularly exercises and takes a break only when his body needs one.

    The workout routine of Kawhi Leonard has been split into two parts, days-wise, and he exercises both in the morning and evening.

    He includes shoulder presses, dumbbell shrugs, dumbbell presses, leg raises, twists, dumbbell curls, hammer curls, and tricep pushdowns on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.

    Likewise, Leonard does lunges, barbell squats, Russian twists, planks, crunches, hamstring leg curls, sit-ups, V-ups, and hanging leg raises on Tuesday and Thursday.

    Speaking about his diet, Leonard follows a strict diet. His meals basically include fruits, vegetables, and animal protein.

    Kawhi avoids processed food, refined sugar, and an excess amount of carbohydrates. Additionally, he does not eat red meat.

    Kawhi Leonard basically eats five times a day. To details, he starts his day with an egg, generally an omelet, some leafy vegetables, and mushrooms.

    Likewise, in between breakfast and lunch, he intakes fruits like avocado and bananas. Sometimes, he prepares a smoothie including those fruits along with vanilla protein and spinach.

    Similarly, he opts for salads made with anything available for lunch, such as beetroots, beans, quinoa, etc. And if Leonard feels like for an evening snack, he munches on a handful of roasted nuts/seeds.

    Finally, for dinner, he often prepares a veg-burger for himself along with a big bowl of salad.

    Career

    Kawhi Anthony Leonard was born in Los Angeles, California, to Mark Leonard and Kim Leonard. He is the youngest of all five siblings and has four elder sisters.

    Being the only male child in the house, he was very close to his father. His father used to own a car wash center named “Compton car wash,” and Leonard used to help him there.

    Sadly, his father was murdered when Kawhi was just 16 years old. To detail, they both were working together at the car wash center they owned when his father was shot on 18th January 2008.

    He went to Canyon Springs High School and later shifted to Martin Luther King High School for his education.

    In 2011, he was drafted by NBA and initially picked by the Indiana Pacers. However, he was later transacted to the San Antonio Spurs, which is the first team he played as a professional basketball player.

    He played for the San Antonio Spurs for seven years, from 2011-2018. Then, Leonard went to play for the NBA team Toronto Raptors for one year. Currently, he is on Los Angeles Clippers, which he joined in 2019.

    Since 2011, Kawhi has proved himself on every challenge life threw at him and earned several recognitions.

    Leonard expresses missing his father and all the precious time they shared when he was alive. Undeniably, his father would be really proud if he were to witness all the success and accolades his son has amassed.

    Personal Life

    Kawhi Leonard lives together with his long-time girlfriend, Kishele Shipley. Both of them attended the State University of San Diego, where they met each other.

    Kishele was a student of Public Administration, and after graduating in 2012, she worked as an HR in San Diego. However, she left everything in San Diego and shifted to Texas to be with Kawhi.

    Both of them try to live a private life and do not share much with the public or the media. Reportedly, the couple is yet to be married.

    Kawhi and Kishele are blessed with two children; a daughter and a son. Their first daughter Kaliyah Leonard was born in October 2016. Nevertheless, the name and other details of the second-born have not been disclosed.
