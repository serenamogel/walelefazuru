    Lauren Zima (born in 1981) is an American Host, Producer, Actress, Model, and Reporter from Los Angeles, California. She currently appears on shows like “Entertainment Tonight” and “ETonline” as a correspondent. Furthermore, the 37-year-old Zima is also one of the co-producers of these shows.

    She interviewed many celebrities like Matt Damon, Reese Witherspoon, Nicole Kidman, Keith Urban, Jennifer Lopez, Gwen Stefani and many more. Moreover, Lauren earlier worked as a host and the Creative Director at Newsy. The reporter was also the managing editor of the Newsy.

    On January 26, 2019, Lauren made her red carpet debut with boyfriend Chris Harrison as a couple. In addition, Chris is an American television and game show host. He is best known for his role as host of “The Bachelor” (ABC reality television dating show) since 2002.

    The love partner posed for pictures together at Entertainment Weekly’s pre-SAG Awards party. The event held at the Chateau Marmont in West Hollywood. In fact, Zima also later posted an Instagram photo of the couple with a heart emoji. She also wrote on her Instagram Story that Harrison “is great at parties”.

    Harrison earlier married to his wife Gwen. The former partner also shares two children, Joshua and Taylor together. However, after 18 years of marriage, they separated in May 2012.
