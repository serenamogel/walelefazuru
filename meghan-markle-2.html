    Meghan Markle Wiki/Biography

    Born on 4 August 1981, Meghan Markle’s age is 41 Years Old as of 2022. She was brought up and raised in an upper-middle-class family from Los Angeles, California, United States. She is known to be an American by nationality and belongs to the Christian religion.

    Career

    Meghan Markle started her career as an actress but initially, she didn’t receive many offers due to her color as she was not suited for black roles and white roles either. She started working as a calligrapher and taught bookbinding to help herself economically.

    After that, she made her first on-screen appearance with her work in the TV serial General Hospital in the year 2002 in which she played a small role of a nurse in a particular episode. After that, she has worked in popular TV shows like Century City, The War at Home, Deal or No Deal, and Fox’s series Fringe and appeared in small supporting roles.

    She has also made her appearance in some popular movies like Get Him to the Greek, Remember Me, The Candidate, Horrible Bosses, and others. In the year 2011, she has received an offer to work in the popular TV show Suits and she worked in the show till the seventh season since the year 2017.

    In the year 2014, Meghan founded a lifestyle blog named The Tig on which she started writing about food, fashion, beauty, traveling, and inspirational women. After three years in the year 2017, she has closed this blog and also deleted all its traces or articles from her different social media handles. But after the success of her blog “The” Tig, she has gained popularity for her fashion sense and style and she has also promoted several popular fashion brands.

    After her engagement and marriage with Prince Harry, she was assigned the duties of Duchess of Sussex. She has made several public appearances and attended various royal events with Prince Harry. She has attended events like the World AIDS Day walkabout in 2017, Commonwealth Day event in 2018, Invictus Games 2018, and other several public and royal events.

    Along with her royal responsibilities she has worked as a philanthropist and works for several social causes. She mainly focuses on empowering women and youth of the nation and joined hands with several non-profitable and charitable organizations. She has traveled across the world for different social causes like Afghanistan and Spain tour with United Service Organization in 2014, Clean Water Campaign in Rwanda, Elephants Without Borders in Botswana, and Project Angel Food in Los Angeles. Between the years 2019 and 2021, she has also worked as the vice-president of The Queen’s Commonwealth Trust.

    Along with her participation in these events, she spoke at different international platforms regarding topics like Black Lives Matter in 2020, Gender Equality and Empowerment of Women, and others. In the year 2020, she and Prince Harry stepped down from their roles as senior members of the royal family. After that, they signed a deal with a media company named Endeavor and start conducting public speaking engagements.

    They have also signed a deal with platforms like Netflix and Spotify to develop series, films, documentaries, and to produce and host their shows on these platforms. They have also invested in a coffee company named Clevr Blends in Southern California.
