    Michael Blakey | Career

    Michael started his career as a music producer when he was 19. He worked as a resident producer in Berlin for Hansa Studios.

    He studied music in Madrid and Berlin. Then he moved to America as a successful drummer, music producer, and music promoter.

    He has produced tracks for successful artists like Boney M, Julio Iglesias, and Englebert Humperdink.

    His talent was recognized by Atico Records while working as a composer and session drummer for Tidal Force. He worked as chief of A&R at Atico Records from 1991 to 1994.

    Later, he started three production companies between 1994 and 1998. One of them was Platinum Records. His company developed the Forensic Frequency Separation Software (FFSS).

    In 1999 he found 2K Sounds along with his partners, Bruce Gladstone and John Guidon.

    Now, he works in his own record company, Electra Star Management.

    Besides music, he’s also involved in TV shows and movie production.
