    Who is Rebel Wilson?

    Rebel Wilson is an Australian stand-up comedian actress and writer who is immensely popular for her work as an actress in the 2012 musical romantic comedy film Pitch Perfect’ whereby, she portrayed the role of Fat Amy. She is also known for being a former Youth Ambassador for Australia based in Southern Africa.

    Rebel Wilson: Early Life, Childhood, and Education

    Rebel Wilson was born on March 2, 1980, to Australian parents in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. Her nationality is Australian and her ethnicity is mixed(English, Irish, Welsh).

    She was brought up by her parents in the suburbs of Kenthurst, Parramatta, and Castle Hill. Her birth name of Melanie Elizabeth Bownds. Her mother’s name is Sue Bownds and her father’s name is unknown.

    She has two sisters: Liberty Wilson and Annachi Wilson. She has a brother named Ryot Wilson. From a very early age of life, she had a sheer interest in acting and other different forms of arts so.

    In which school did Rebel Wilson attend?

    For the sake of her fostering interest in acting, she attended the University of New South Wales. There, she completed her Bachelor’s degree in Arts (Theatre and Performance Studies) along with her Bachelor’s degree in Law Studies during the year of 2009.

    Prior to joining the University of New South Wales, she attended Tara Anglican School for Girls and graduated in 1997.

    Rebel Wilson: Professional Life and Career

    Rebel Wilson initiated her career after being trained at the Australian Theatre for Young People (ATYP) and ‘The Second city’. After a lot of attempts, Wilson got the opportunity to work as an actress for her debut film ‘Fat Pizza’ whereby, she played the role of Toula.

    In the same year, she got another opportunity to work as an actress for the 2003 SBS Television series ‘Pizza’. Then, she got her first breakthrough to work for a Hollywood movie ‘Bridesmaids’ in which she played the role of Brynn followed by her work for the CBS sitcom ‘Rules of Engagement and the Comedy Central series, ‘Workaholics’. Now, in 2012, she got her second and major breakthrough for the role of ‘Fat Amy’ in the 2012 musical romantic comedy film Pitch Perfect.

    She received Best Supporting Actress from the San Diego Film Critics Society and Best Actress in a Comedy from the Broadcast Film Critics Association along with some other reputed awards. She also worked for ‘Pitch Perfect 2’ along with an opportunity to work as an actress for ‘Pitch Perfect 3’ which is under production.
