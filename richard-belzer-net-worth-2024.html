    Early Life and Career Beginnings

    Richard Belzer was born in Bridgeport, Connecticut, and grew up in a Jewish family. He began his career in the entertainment industry as a reporter for the Bridgeport Post. His career took a turn when he moved to New York City in the early 1970s and began doing stand-up comedy at the Improv.

    Acting Career

    Belzer’s acting career took off in the 1990s when he was cast as Detective John Munch in the TV series “Homicide: Life on the Street.” He reprised this role in “Law & Order: Special Victims Unit,” which significantly contributed to his net worth.

    Law & Order: Special Victims Unit

    Belzer’s role in “Law & Order: Special Victims Unit” is one of his most notable contributions to his net worth. He played the character Detective John Munch for 15 seasons, making him one of the longest-serving cast members on the show.

    Other Acting Roles

    Stand-up Comedy

    Writing Career

    Real Estate Investments
