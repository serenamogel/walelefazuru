    I can’t believe that January is almost over. I mean, really, where has the month gone? My boys are already picking out their Valentines for school friends and I’m thinking, wait, what? Valentine’s Day? Already?

    As fast as January has gone by, it’s been a month of taking time for myself and I’ve been LOVING it. I made a whole bunch of New Year’s Resolutions this year and while I haven’t stuck to all of them, I’m doing good. Really good!

    Now you all know I have a sweet tooth that never quits. I am always looking for ways to skinny up recipes that I love. The other night we all went out to dinner at Chicago Fire Pizza and then walked across the street to Rick’s Dessert Diner. Yeah. That was one of those days that I didn’t do so well lol!

    Bryce picked out a 6-layer Red Velvet Cake for his dessert and after taking just one bite, I was pretty sure I had died and gone to heaven. Intense. Amazing. And I wanted it again for breakfast. So, instead of eating cake for breakfast, I made this Skinny Red Velvet Shake. It was awesome!

    I started with Greek frozen yogurt (vanilla or chocolate will do) and added some red velvet cake mix, a scoop of protein powder (chocolate for me!), and Blue Diamond Almondmilk Cashewmilk blend for seriously amazing flavor. Honestly you guys, if you haven’t had a chance to try this milk yet – now is the time. It is absolutely delicious and comes in regular, vanilla, and unsweetened varieties. I used the regular but the unsweetened or vanilla would be terrific too!

    Now the thing that is so special about red velvet cake is the cream cheese frosting – it’s the perfect complement! I had to incorporate it somehow and came up with this cream cheese whipped cream that is guaranteed to knock your socks off! I’m going to be putting this stuff on everything.

    So if you’re one of those people that are sticking to your New Year’s Resolutions like glue – good for you! Keep up the good work and when you’re thinking of walking on the wild side – give this shake a try! It would make a really delicious and lighter alternative for Valentine’s Day as well!

    Skinny Red Velvet Shake

    This Skinny Red Velvet Shake is the perfect treat for Valentine's Day or whenever you need a little something special. Topped with a decadent cream cheese whipped cream, this skinny shake is hard to resist! Great for breakfast or dessert!

    Course Dessert Cuisine American Keyword red velvet, red velvet shake Prep Time 7 minutes minutes Total Time 7 minutes minutes Servings 2 servings Calories 296kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 1 cup frozen yogurt I used Greek
    • 3/4 cup Blue Diamond Almond Breeze Almondmilk Cashewmilk blend
    • 1/4 cup Red Velvet cake mix
    • 1 scoop of protein powder optional, use chocolate or vanilla

    Cream Cheese Whipped Cream

    • 2 oz light cream cheese softened
    • 1/2 heavy whipping cream
    • 2 tbsp powdered sugar
    • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

    Instructions

    • Blend frozen yogurt, almondmilk, cake mix and protein powder together until smooth.

    • Whip cream cheese, and heavy whipping cream together until stiff peaks form.

    • Beat in powdered sugar and vanilla extract.

    • Pour shake into two glasses and top with whipped cream.

    • Serve with sprinkles!
