    It’s tradition for me to buy a new pair of sneakers every time the weather starts to warm up, and here in Maryland, the temperature has started to climb. You may think this sounds unrealistic, but I wear the sneakers I buy to the ground. They’re usually scuffed beyond repair, the rubber outsole worn down, and the insole is pretty much flat. When replacing my everyday sneakers, I go for a style that can be dressed up or down, is super comfortable, and can hold up till next year, and my recent pickup of the Veja Campo Sneakers checks all those boxes and now I’m considering a second pair. 

    Veja Campo Sneakers

    The sneakers come in 19 colorways including white and pink, white and black, white and blue, tan and white, with sizes ranging from 4 through 13. They feature a classic lace-up design, a cushioned tongue and ankle collar for added comfort, 1-inch rubber soles, and a contrasting-colored brand emblem on the side of the shoe. The insoles are also removable, and the rounded toe box offers plenty of space.

    I understand why Veja shoes are the go-to for Kate Middleton and Meghan Markle: They are comfortable right out of the box. The soles are plush, so I’m able to walk in them for hours without my feet feeling fatigued, and they offer a good amount of arch support for my virtually flat foot. They come in a ton of colors and have a minimalistic design that would pair well with everything from jeans to a dress. The quality matches up with the price tag and the outer shell and rubber sole feel super durable, which is what I need from shoes I plan on wearing on a daily basis. These are great year-round shoes because the lining is breathable yet insulating so my toes aren’t chilled on colder days.

    Not only are the Campo Sneakers supportive, but they’re super versatile when it comes to styling. I’m currently styling them with jeans and slouchy off-the-shoulder sweaters, but they’ve also looked great with a silk maxi skirt and graphic tee. As we move deeper into spring, I know these will also pair well with a breezy mini dress or linen matching set. I love my current pair so much I’m even planning to go back for another shade.
